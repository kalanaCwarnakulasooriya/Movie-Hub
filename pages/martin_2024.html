Plot Summary

Martin is a 2024 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by A. J. K. S. and produced by A. J. K. S. and A. J. K. S.. The film stars Dhanush, Rashmika Mandanna, and Prakash Raj in lead roles, with a supporting cast including Samuthirakani, and others. The film's music is composed by Anirudh Ravichander, and the cinematography is handled by V. Ramachandran. The story revolves around a group of criminals who plan to rob a bank, but are caught by the police. With high-octane action sequences, emotional depth, and a gripping narrative, Martin is set to be a thrilling cinematic experience. The film is expected to be released on 11 October 2024.
